Tips and Suggestions

What to look for before trusting a link

!

Check the full domain

Brand words in the path or subdomain can be misleading. The real hostname matters most.

i

Urgency is a common lure

Reset now, verify now, payment failed, and account locked are common pressure tactics.

✓

Verify separately

If a link feels off, visit the official site directly instead of trusting the message.

Examples

https://example.com

A normal HTTPS example for a low-risk check.

http://192.168.1.5/login

An IP-based hostname that helps demonstrate why raw numeric hosts can look suspicious.

https://pay-update-secure-login.top/a/b/c/d/%2Freset?next=home

A higher-risk example with unusual wording, path depth, and a risky ending.
